the bitcoin network in realtime

Bitcoin transactions are recorded on a crypto-ledger called the block chain. Transactions from anywhere in the world appear on the network and are shared from peer to peer, before being eventually confirmed and included in the block chain.

We're visualizing transactions in real time as they appear on the network and get confirmed into blocks. We were curious to visualize dynamics. How much bitcoin does each transaction represent? How frequently do they appear? We want to create intuitive, visual representations that bring the system to life.
